About us

Welcome to Christian Faith Family Church, your online sanctuary for spiritual connection. Join our diverse community, where faith is shared, and love guides our journey.

Prayer gatherings

Tuesdays

Join us for a sacred moment of collective prayer. In our Prayer Circle, hearts converge in shared hopes and supplications. Let's uplift each other, transcending boundaries to create a spiritual bond. All are welcome to join, seeking solace, guidance, and divine blessings.

Youth and family fellowship.

Thursdays

Gather your family for a unique blend of fun and faith in our Youth & Family Fellowship. It's a joyous space where all generations connect, sharing laughter, stories, and the wisdom found in our faith. Join us for enriching activities that strengthen family bonds and nurture the spiritual growth of our youth. Together, let's build a foundation of grace that lasts a lifetime.

Bible study

Saturdays

Delve into the richness of divine teachings with our Bible Study sessions. In a welcoming virtual setting, we journey through sacred scriptures, unraveling layers of wisdom and understanding. Join fellow seekers in thoughtful discussions, gain new insights, and deepen your connection with the Word. Let Scripture Explorations be a source of inspiration and growth on your spiritual path.

Graceful Sundays

Sundays

Experience the essence of spirituality with "Graceful Sundays," our main event that seamlessly blends uplifting worship with powerful preaching. Join us for a soul-enriching journey where heartfelt worship meets insightful sermons, creating a space for reflection, connection, and spiritual growth. Let our Sunday gatherings be a source of inspiration that resonates throughout your week.

Get involved!

We invite you to be an active part of our digital congregation. Engage in discussions, share your thoughts, and participate in our various online events. Whether you are a longtime believer or exploring spirituality for the first time, there's a place for you at Christian Faith Family Church. Thank you for being a part of our online church family. Together, let's continue to spread love, kindness, and the teachings that unite us.
